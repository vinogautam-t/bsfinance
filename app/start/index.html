Get Started

Step 1. Open an account on BSFinance.biz
  1. Fill in all the required fields of the application form on the Sign up page.
  2. Submit your registration by clicking on 'Open my account'.
  3. After that you will be able to log into your account and make your first investment.
Step 2. Make deposit

In order to make a deposit you need to have an account in any of the following payment systems: Perfect Money, Payeer or cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin, Litecoin, Dash, Ethereum or Bitcoin Cash.

To exchange your funds to Perfect Money or other electronic currencies you may check lists of Certified Partners of Perfect Money.

  1. Go to Make deposit page.
  2. Choose desired investment plan, fill in desired amount of deposit, choose electronic currency and click on 'Make deposit' button.
    If you want to make a deposit using your account balance choose Payment processor from 'Funding from the account balance'.
  3. On the next page you need to confirm your deposit by clicking 'Process'.
  4. After that you will be redirected to desired Payment processor. Then follow the instructions of Payment processor.
Step 3. Withdraw your funds
  1. Go to Withdraw page.
  2. Then choose payment processor and fill in the amount that you want to withdraw and click 'Withdraw'.
How to make deposit using cryptocurrencies?

BSFinance Limited works with cryptocurrencies and you may make deposit using cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Litecoin, Dash, Ethereum or Bitcoin Cash.

To create your Bitcoin address you can use, for instance, blockchain.info or you may also use original bitcoin node which you may download from Bitcoin.org or use Electrum for bitcoin and the same but for litecoin - Electrum for litecoin.

To make a deposit at BSFinance Limited via Bitcoin/Litecoin/Dash/Ethereum/Bitcoin Cash you need to:

  1. Go to Make deposit page.
  2. Choose desired investment plan, fill in desired amount of deposit, choose 'Bitcoin/Litecoin/Dash/Ethereum/Bitcoin Cash' and click 'Make a deposit'.
  3. Send displayed amount of BTC on our address that you find on the next page.
